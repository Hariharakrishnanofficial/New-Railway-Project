Generate All 3 At Once:
─────────────────────

  python3 << 'EOF'
  import secrets
  print("SECRET_KEY=" + secrets.token_hex(32))
  print("SESSION_SECRET=" + secrets.token_hex(64))
  print("JWT_SECRET_KEY=" + secrets.token_hex(64))
  EOF

  Output looks like:
  SECRET_KEY=a7f8e9d2c1b4f5g6h7i8j9k0l1m2n3o4p5q6r7s8
  SESSION_SECRET=b9f8e7d6c5a4f3g2h1i0j9k8l7m6n5o4p3q2r1s0t9u8v7w6x5y4z3...
  JWT_SECRET_KEY=c1f0e9d8c7b6a5f4g3h2i1j0k9l8m7n6o5p4q3r2s1t0u9v8w7x6y5z4...
